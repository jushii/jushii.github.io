About

Hello! I'm a Unity certified game developer from Finland with a focus on design and gameplay programming. My key strength is to quickly transform a game concept into a playable prototype.

I've developed games for various platforms such as PC, Mac, iOS, Android, HTC Vive and Oculus Rift. My latest project, Flail Rider, gained over 2 million downloads on App Store and Google Play.
