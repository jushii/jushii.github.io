About

Hello! I'm a Finnish game developer specializing in Unity and C#. I love building my own systems, but I'm no stranger to popular plugins and libraries such as UniRX, DOTween and I2 Localization. I value clean code, but also appreciate the chaotic process known as game jams. I've worked remote and non-remote, in teams of two or over fifty. I understand the importance of game feel and juice. Version control is my second best friend.

Besides commercial games I've worked on, check out other projects I've developed in school and during my personal time.
